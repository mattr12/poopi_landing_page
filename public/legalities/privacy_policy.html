Privacy Policy for Poopi

Last Updated: August 20, 2024

Welcome to Poopi! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app. By using Poopi, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information: - Email Address: Collected when you create an account. - User Profile: Includes your email and any other optional information you provide, such as first or last name.

b. Pet Information: - Pet name, breed, birthdate, and other attributes that you can optionally add: Collected to provide insights.

c. Log Data: - Poop Logs: Includes date, time, frequency, consistency, colors, and other characteristics of your pet’s poop. - Influencing Factors: Includes diet, activity level, medications, supplements, and many other factors that may play a role in a pet’s regularity or irregularity.

d. Device Information: - Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

a. To Provide and Improve Our Services: - Use your email and profile information to create and manage your account. - Use pet, log, and factor data to offer insights and track trends, as well as statistics that may help users monitor the health of their pet.

b. To Facilitate Sharing: - Allow you to share your pet’s logs and data with other users.

c. For Communication: - Send you updates, notifications, and other relevant communications.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

a. With Other Users: - Information you choose to share will be visible to other users.

b. With Third Parties: - We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except to trusted third parties who assist us in operating our app, conducting our business, or serving our users, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

c. Legal Requirements: - We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Please only share data within the app that is not sensitive.

5. Your Data Rights

a. Access and Update: - You can access and update your personal information through your account settings.

b. Deletion: - You can request the deletion of your account and personal information by deleting your account through the app.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.

By using Poopi, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
